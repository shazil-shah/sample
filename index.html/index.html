smart lighting

LDR‑based automation

The system continuously monitors ambient light using LDR sensors. As dusk falls or clouds pass, the detected drop in lux triggers the LED array – instant, autonomous illumination without human intervention. Energy waste becomes a thing of the past.

rain detection

medows protection

A dedicated rain sensor module installed in the medows area detects water droplets. Upon rainfall, the system instantly activates protective responses — covering vents, retracting exposed components — ensuring infrastructure durability.

digital logic core

Built with comparator circuits and flip‑flops, the rain detector sends a clean digital signal to the actuator network. No microcontroller needed — pure digital logic, just like our DLD course.
